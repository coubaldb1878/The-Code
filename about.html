ABOUT US

At The Pie Pizzeria, we believe great pizza brings people together. Located in Eau Claire, we’re a locally owned pizzeria focused on serving fresh ingredients, handcrafted pizzas, and a welcoming atmosphere for everyone who walks through our doors. From classic favorites to creative specialty pies, every pizza is made with care and baked fresh to order. Whether you’re stopping in with friends, grabbing takeout after work, or enjoying a family dinner, The Pie is all about good food, good people, and bringing a little extra flavor to the Eau Claire community.
